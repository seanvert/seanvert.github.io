flow

phenomenology of enjoyment

1

the experience usually occurs when we confront tasks we have a chance of completing

2

we must be able to concentrate on what we are doing

3/4

concentration is usually possible because the task undertaken has clear goals and provides immediate feedback

5

one acts with a deep but effortless involvement that removes from awareness the worries and frustrations of everyday life

6

enjoyable experiences allow people to exercise a sense of control over their actions

7

concent for the self disappears, yet paradoxically the sense of self emerges stronger after the flow experience is over

8

the sense of duration of time is altered; hours pass by in minutes, and minutes can stretch out to seem like hours.
